Apna niche (jaise "personal finance" ya "fitness"), audience location, platform aur traffic size bharein. "Find Affiliate Opportunities" par click karein — AI aapke liye best-fit affiliate programs ki list banayega, har ek ke saath commission rate, cookie duration aur fit score.

2. Fit Score kya hai?

High Fit ka matlab hai ye program aapke niche/audience ke liye bahut suitable hai. Medium aur Low fit wale programs bhi kaam kar sakte hain, lekin thoda kam relevant hain.
